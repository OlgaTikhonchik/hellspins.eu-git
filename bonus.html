How Cashback Works

Weekly cashback is a loss‑rebate applied to your results for a fixed weekly window. The system totals your eligible stakes and subtracts what you won back in the same window, along with any reversals or voided rounds. If the figure is negative, a percentage of that loss is returned to you.
